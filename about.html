Our story

Pawsitive Futures is a non-profit animal rescue focused on giving cats, dogs, and bunnies a second chance at life. Through rescue, foster care, and community support, we connect animals in need with loving forever homes.

In addition to adoption services, we are committed to educating new pet owners by offering helpful resources, guidance, and ongoing support to ensure every adoption is a lasting success.

Whether you’re looking to adopt, volunteer, foster, or donate, you are helping us build a brighter future — one paw at a time.

Our team

Our Team

Our work is powered by a passionate group of animal lovers, volunteers, and foster caregivers who are dedicated to making a difference in the lives of animals every day.

Our Mission

Our mission is to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome animals in need while promoting responsible pet ownership. We strive to create a compassionate community where every animal is treated with care, respect, and love.

Our Vision

We envision a future where every animal has a safe, permanent home and no pet is left behind. Through education, community partnerships, and ongoing support, we aim to reduce animal homelessness and build a more humane world.
